• Fred the Baker Baking Company

    Welcome to Fred the Baker Baking Company! Fred needs our help because it is always "Time to make the donuts". This game uses button clicks to create virtual donuts. One click on the donut button makes a new donut. Interested in maximizing your donut production? Auto Clickers will click the donut button for you. Donut multipliers will increase the value of each click! These tools will get more powerful with each purchase, however the cost will also increase. Have fun and thanks for helping Fred the Baker Baking Company.
